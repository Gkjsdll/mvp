Getting Started

How to play

  1. Click in the box to set your username
  2. Click in the box to see how close you are to the target
  3. Use how close your previous guesses were to locate the target
  4. Repeat step 3 until you win!

Scoring

  • Your score starts at 10 000
  • You lose points every time you click
  • You lose points the longer you take to win
  • Scores are automatically submitted to the leaderboard
